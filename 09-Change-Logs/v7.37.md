## Safety

- Internal run does not call providers, read key values, perform external training, replace runtime packages, update Skills, or publish Skills.
- Training outputs remain metadata-only and do not expose raw case material, raw OCR text, secrets, provider responses, or local paths.
- No final legal opinion, formal report, PDF/DOCX, public link, email, or external delivery is generated.
