## Safety

- Candidate packs are metadata-only, owner-only, source-traced, and audited.
- Triaged v7.31h feedback and risk metadata may become iteration candidates, but they do not mutate loaded packages or lawyer-approved packages.
- Disable, rollback, runtime policy changes, training, Skill updates, Skill publishing, final legal opinions, final reports, public links, email, and external delivery remain blocked.

## Deferred

- Rebuilding a next experience package draft from a ready candidate pack is deferred to v7.31j.
- Any rebuilt package must later return to the practice load review boundary before runtime loading.
