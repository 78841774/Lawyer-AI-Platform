## Safety

- Fact previews remain metadata-only and draft-only.
- Owner correction is gated and does not store raw correction content.
- Fact version timeline is owner-only metadata.
- Fact quality and gate status are reference-only.
- Legal analysis input readiness does not auto-trigger legal analysis.
- No real provider calls.
- No API key access or frontend key display.
- No real case material reading.
- No raw content, local paths, secrets, final fact findings, final legal opinions, final reports, real PDF/DOCX files, email, public links, Skill publishing, training writes, or external delivery.
