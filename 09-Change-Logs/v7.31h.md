## Safety

- Observations store redacted output summaries only.
- Lawyer feedback does not mutate loaded packages.
- Risk events do not automatically disable or rollback packages.
- Feedback classification may recommend disable, rollback, or iteration candidate creation, but those actions are not executed automatically.
- No provider call, API key read, raw output return, raw material exposure, Skill update, Skill publish, real training, final legal opinion, final report, public link, email, or external delivery is performed.
