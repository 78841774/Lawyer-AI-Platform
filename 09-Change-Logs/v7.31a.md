## Safety

- v7.31a prepares training material metadata only and does not execute Codex training.
- Open or unresolved cases do not participate in training.
- Raw content, raw OCR content, API keys, secret values, local paths, and identity values are not returned.
- Redaction preserves legally necessary metadata such as jurisdiction context, age/capacity context, subject type, contract type, object type, timeline markers, and evidence type.
- v7.31a does not write training sets, update Skills, publish Skills, generate final legal opinions, generate final reports, create public links, send email, or trigger external delivery.
