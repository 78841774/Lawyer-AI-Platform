## Safety

- Live providers remain disabled by default.
- Live provider eligibility requires explicit confirmation.
- Provider adapter gaps return gated / adapter-unavailable metadata.
- API keys are not read into frontend responses and are not displayed.
- Owner downloads are owner-only metadata; no public link is created.
- No email, third-party upload, client delivery, or external delivery is triggered.
- Open cases do not create training data.
- Open cases do not write to training sets.
- Open cases do not update or publish Skills.
- Raw case material and OCR text are not written to Git, docs, Project Context, diagnostics, regression output, or public showcase pages.
- Outputs remain draft-only and source-trace-required.
- Lawyer review and final lock remain required.
- No final legal opinion, final report, formal report, real final file delivery, email, or external delivery is generated.
