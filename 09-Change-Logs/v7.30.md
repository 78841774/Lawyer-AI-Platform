## Safety

- Codex training is not model fine-tuning.
- v7.30 does not execute real training.
- v7.30 does not read real case materials, raw OCR, API keys, secret values, or local sensitive paths.
- v7.30 does not use open cases for training.
- v7.30 does not update or publish Skills.
- v7.30 does not generate final legal opinions, final reports, real files, public links, email, or external delivery.
