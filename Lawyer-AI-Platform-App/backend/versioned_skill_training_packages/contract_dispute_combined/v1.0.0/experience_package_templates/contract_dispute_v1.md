# contract_dispute_combined Experience Package Template

Status: readonly experience package template candidate.

## Purpose

This template combines `case-fact-extractor-v3` and `case-analysis-pro-v3` for future contract dispute training.

It is not a published Skill and does not replace `skill_001` or `skill_002`.

## Package Flow

1. Folder-aware material intake.
2. Fact extraction and material flow analysis.
3. Evidence matrix and amount audit.
4. Admission gate.
5. E1, 00, A1-A13, E legal analysis chain.
6. A10 disputed-issue deep analysis.
7. Evaluation and human review.
