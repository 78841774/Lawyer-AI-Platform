# case-analysis-pro-v3 Litigation Strategy Rules

Source basis: `case-analysis-pro-v3/SKILL.md` and `references/lawyer-output-standards.md`.

Status: readonly legacy asset prepared for versioned training packages.

## Strategy Requirements

Every disputed issue must include:

* primary route,
* fallback route,
* lowest acceptable route,
* likely opposing argument,
* judge's likely question,
* evidence reinforcement task,
* risk and next action.

## Step Placement

* A5 designs evidence strategy.
* A10 deepens disputed legal issues.
* A11 converts A10 into hearing operations.
* A12 converts A10 into advocacy argument.
* A13 converts the result into client-facing closing or stage report.
