# case-fact-extractor-v3 Legal Element Mapping Rules

Source basis: `case-fact-extractor-v3/SKILL.md`.

Status: readonly legacy asset prepared for versioned training packages.

## Purpose

Fact extraction must prepare structured inputs for A4 claim or defense analysis and A10 disputed-issue deep analysis.

It must not replace legal analysis.

## Required Mapping

Each material fact should be mapped to:

* Possible claim or defense element.
* Supporting evidence.
* Missing evidence.
* Favorable side.
* Risk note.
* Later use in A4 or A10.

## Admission Conclusion

At the end of the fact report, state whether the case is:

* admitted to full A-series analysis,
* temporarily limited to preliminary legal judgment,
* or blocked pending supplementation.
