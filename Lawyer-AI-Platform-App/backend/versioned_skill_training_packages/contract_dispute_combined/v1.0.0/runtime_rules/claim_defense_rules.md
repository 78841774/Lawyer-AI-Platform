# case-analysis-pro-v3 Claim and Defense Rules

Source basis: `case-analysis-pro-v3/SKILL.md`, `references/depth-standards.md`, and `references/scoring.md`.

Status: readonly legacy asset prepared for versioned training packages.

## A4 Boundary

A4 is `请求权 / 抗辩权基础`.

It must decompose:

* right source,
* legal elements,
* burden of proof,
* corresponding facts,
* existing evidence,
* gaps,
* opposing arguments,
* court judgment path,
* probability and reinforcement tasks.

It must not draft pleadings or advocacy briefs.

## Relationship with A10

A4 supplies legal element mapping to A10. A10 then deepens disputed issues through practice, theory, comparison strategy, and judge review.
