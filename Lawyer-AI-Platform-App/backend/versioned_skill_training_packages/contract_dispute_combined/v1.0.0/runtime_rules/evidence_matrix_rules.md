# case-fact-extractor-v3 Evidence Matrix Rules

Source basis: `case-fact-extractor-v3/SKILL.md` and `references/evidence-processing.md`.

Status: readonly legacy asset prepared for versioned training packages.

## Required Matrix

Every key fact must be mapped to evidence.

| fact | evidence | original excerpt | purpose | probative force | risk | reinforcement |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| To be filled per case | To be filled per case | To be filled per case | To be filled per case | S/A/B/C | To be filled per case | To be filled per case |

## Evidence Strength

* S: original, admission, notarized or timestamped electronic data, court or administrative document.
* A: complete chat record, signed contract, payment voucher, invoice, platform backend data.
* B: screenshot, photo, screen recording, unilateral table that can be cross-checked.
* C: unilateral statement, unverified screenshot, indirect material without a complete chain.

## Court Use

The matrix must prepare facts for later A4 and A10 use. It must not produce final legal conclusions.
