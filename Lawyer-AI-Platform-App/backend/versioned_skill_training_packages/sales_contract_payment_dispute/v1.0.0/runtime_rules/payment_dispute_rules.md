# payment_dispute Runtime Rules

Status: readonly case-cause runtime rules.

## Focus

* Contract price and payment schedule.
* Delivery and acceptance as payment condition.
* Amount audit, partial payment, refund, setoff, and reconciliation.
* Interest, penalty, and late-payment loss.
* Evidence chain for invoices, delivery notes, statements, bank flow, and chat admissions.

These rules supplement parent package rules and cannot disable safety gates.
